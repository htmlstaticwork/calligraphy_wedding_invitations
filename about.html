✦ How It Began

A Passion for
Beautiful Paper

WedINv was founded in 2014 by Ananya Krishnan, a calligrapher and paper artist who believed that wedding invitations should be the first chapter of a love story — not just a card in an envelope.

What began as a small studio in Chennai has grown into an internationally recognised atelier, trusted by couples across four continents. Yet our heart remains the same: every piece is crafted by hand, with intention and artistry.

  • Hand calligraphy on every suite
  • Sourced from finest paper mills globally
  • Certified by the IAMPETH Calligraphy Society
  • Featured in Vogue Weddings & Harper's Bazaar Bride
